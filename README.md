Hey there, I have analysed telco customer's dataset and try to predict that whether customer will churn or not? 
if we know a certain group of customers will churn, we might send things to them for free and make them stay with us maybe.
Moreover, with EDA of this project, we get a chance to know our customers better.
I have tried a few models and found out that LogisticRegression is better even though I have tried more complex model. 
This could be because of dataset being very small around 7000 rows.
After finding out LogisticRegression is the model I will continue with, I have hyperparameter tuned my model with respect to recall value. 
I have chosen recall my targer score since it will allow me to maximize True Positives and make less False Negatives which is pretty 
important since we don't want to loose customers. However, there is a trade&off between precision and recall meaning in order to make more accurate predictions
on customers which will churn, we need to make more sacrifices on customer's that won't churn however, we tagged them as they will churn.
Which is problematic actually. If we send free stuff to customers our model have tagges churn, we will loose resources for nothing.
